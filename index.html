Dallas's Premier Auto Body & Collision Repair

The Crash Factory is a full-service auto body shop and collision repair center located on South R L Thornton Freeway in Dallas, Texas. We're the go-to destination for drivers across the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex who need quality bodywork, precision paint matching, frame straightening, and complete vehicle restoration.

Whether you've been in a minor fender bender or a major collision, our experienced technicians have the skills and equipment to restore your vehicle to factory condition. We use state-of-the-art computerized color matching, precision frame alignment equipment, and premium materials to ensure every repair meets the highest standards.

Unlike most shops, we're open 24/7 — because accidents don't happen on a schedule. We welcome all insurance carriers and handle the entire claims process on your behalf.

Beyond standard auto body repair, The Crash Factory specializes in classic car restoration, heavy equipment and industrial machinery paint, fleet vehicle bodywork, and custom paint jobs. If it has a body, we can fix it.

Common Repairs We Handle

  • Front-end and rear-end collision repair
  • Side panel dent removal and replacement
  • Bumper repair and replacement
  • Hail damage repair and paintless dent removal (PDR)
  • Full vehicle paint and custom color jobs
  • Frame damage assessment and straightening
  • Classic and vintage car full restoration
  • Heavy equipment and industrial machinery paint
  • Fleet vehicle bodywork and maintenance
  • Glass replacement coordination
  • Headlight and taillight restoration
